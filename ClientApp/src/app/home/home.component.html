Home School Class


An easy way to start Home Schooling!


To help you get started, there are example schedules & a list of FREE online courses.



Get Started


Review the list of courses and visit the provided addresses to get an idea of what is available.


Then choose what is right courses for your home school.

The online addresses provided are intended to meet the minimum necessary requirements for the core subjects. There may be other requirements in your State, or you may decide to include classes not listed.


After deciding on a class schedule for your favorite Home Scholar, please add the schedule to the Students list for others to follow your example.
